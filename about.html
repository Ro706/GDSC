About SIT

Symbiosis Institute of Technology (SIT) is a constituent college of Symbiosis International University, Pune. It is located in Nagpur, Maharashtra. The institute offers a B.Tech Computer Science and Engineering programme. The institute has a sprawling campus with state-of-the-art infrastructure and facilities. The faculty members are highly qualified and experienced. The institute has a well-stocked library with a vast collection of books, journals, and research papers. The institute also has a computer center with the latest hardware and software. The institute has a placement cell that helps students get placed in top companies. The cell organizes various training programs, workshops, and seminars to enhance the skills of the students. The institute also has various clubs and societies that organize various cultural and technical events throughout the year
