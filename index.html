About GDSC

GDSC stands for Google Developer Student Clubs.
It is a global community of university students who
are passionate about technology an
GDSC clubs are led by students who are interested in
growing the community and helping other students learn
about Google technologies.

About SIT

Symbiosis Institute of Technology Nagpur (SIT Nagpur)
is a private engineering institute located in Nagpur,Maharashtra,
India.It was established in 2021 and is a constituent
college of Symbiosis International University, Nagpur.
The institute offers a B.Tech program in Computer Science
and Engineering, with specializations in AI and Machine
Learning, Data Science,and Cloud Computing.
