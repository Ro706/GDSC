About GDSC

Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC) is an initiative taken by Google Developers to help university students learn developing for Mobile and the Web. Specifically aimed for university students to help them build their mobile, web, machine learning, cloud service skills. GDSC is like your college/ university clubs, powered by Google Developers. Every year, Google selects leads from across the globe who align with the vision of GDSC and want to empower their community. You can explore more than 1,900 college and university chapters across more than 100 countries. Meet and learn with other budding developers and build solutions for local businesses and communities with Google technology. The program helps students bridge the gap between theory and practice in understanding the intersections between social groups, technology and user behavior.

About SIT

Symbiosis Institute of Technology (SIT) is a constituent college of Symbiosis International University, Pune. It is located in Nagpur, Maharashtra. The institute offers a B.Tech Computer Science and Engineering programme. The institute has a sprawling campus with state-of-the-art infrastructure and facilities. The faculty members are highly qualified and experienced. The institute has a well-stocked library with a vast collection of books, journals, and research papers. The institute also has a computer center with the latest hardware and software. The institute has a placement cell that helps students get placed in top companies. The cell organizes various training programs, workshops, and seminars to enhance the skills of the students. The institute also has various clubs and societies that organize various cultural and technical events throughout the year
